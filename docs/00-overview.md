# 00 — Overview brand

> Source : Brand book v1.4, pages 1–10 + manifesto.

## Positionnement

> *We're the leading global employee benefits and engagement partner that opens up* **a world of opportunities** *to help people enjoy more of what really matters in their lives.*

Le "**X**" est le symbole central : il représente l'infini des opportunités et connecte les 4 audiences :

| Audience | Promesse |
|---|---|
| **Consumer** | *To enjoy more of what really matters.* Solutions personnalisées qui apportent liberté et enrichissement à la vie des salariés. |
| **Merchant** | *To enable more dynamic business growth.* Connexion avec les consommateurs + business additionnel et durable. |
| **Client** (entreprise) | *To develop more meaningful relationships.* Aider les clients à mieux s'engager avec leurs employés. |
| **Our employees** | *To meaningfully inspire and grow your career.* Développement professionnel + impact sur des millions de vies. |

## Manifesto (résumé)

> We are Pluxee, your global employee engagement and benefits partner. We're opening up a world of opportunities to help people enjoy more of what really matters in their lives.
>
> Building on Sodexo's rich heritage, we're on a new path with big ambitions for the future. **500 000 clients, 1,7 million de marchands, 36 millions de consommateurs dans 31 pays.**

## Personnalité de marque — 4 piliers

| Trait | Mantra |
|---|---|
| **Optimistic** | We see the potential in everything and everyone, and we're constantly striving to do and be better. |
| **Relentless** | With our progressive and visionary attitude, we're on a mission to understand what truly matters. |
| **Considerate** | We are instinctively inclusive, appreciative of people's unique differences and our responsibility to communities and the planet. |
| **Dedicated** | We're a reliable partner that always acts with integrity and can be trusted to do the right thing. |

## Le nom "Pluxee"

- **plux** = "plus", évoque l'ouverture, le "+" d'opportunités.
- **ee** = "employee engagement" / "employee experience" — la catégorie de Pluxee.
- **X** = symbolise les expériences personnalisées et durables offertes aux clients, marchands, consommateurs et employés.
